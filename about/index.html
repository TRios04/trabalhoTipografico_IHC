About & license

This project was made by the exploration of the typographic design in a web application.

Typography in web design is crucial for creating a compelling and user-friendly interface. It involves careful selection and arrangement of fonts, spacing, and text to establish a consistent visual identity. The goal is to effectively communicate information through graphic composition, ensuring the chosen font, contrast, visual hierarchy, and spacing are balanced and easy to read. As it's significant impact on user experience and brand identity, it's essential to invest time and resources to find the right fit. It also contributes to the overall structure and hierarchy of the content, and the alignment of text is another important aspect of typography.

Such a design creates the foundation for choosing and defining the characteristics of fonts, spacing and text with the aim of creating a consistent and quality visual identity. Therefore, your goal is to establish effective communication that conveys, through graphic composition, the best font, adequate contrast, the proposed visual hierarchy and conscious spacing.
